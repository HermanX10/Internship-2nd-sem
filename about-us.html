Our Story

Celebrating connections through thoughtful gifting since 2015

Who We Are

GiftNest was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to make gifting meaningful again. In a world of mass-produced presents, we wanted to bring back the thoughtfulness and personal touch that makes gift-giving special.

What started as a small boutique in Mumbai has grown into a beloved national brand, but our commitment to quality and personal service remains unchanged. We carefully curate each item in our collection, working directly with artisans and suppliers who share our values.

Our Values

Thoughtfulness

Every gift is selected with care and attention to detail, ensuring it conveys your feelings perfectly.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly materials and ethical sourcing in all our products.

Community

We support local artisans and small businesses through our supply chain.

Quality

Only the finest materials and craftsmanship make it into our collections.
